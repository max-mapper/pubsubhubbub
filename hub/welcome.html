Welcome to the Psychic Warlock PubSubHubbub hub

This is a fork of the public Google hub that has Cross Origin Resource Sharing enabled. This hub was built to enable OStatus applications to talk to each other, namely Monocles. Feel free to use it!

Hub debug

From here you can,

  • Subscribe to a feed or debug your subscriber (with stats!).
  • Publish a feed or debug your published feeds.
  • Learn about Pubsubhubbub
